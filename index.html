OUR STORY

Here's where we tell you how we met and fell in love. Skip this part if you don't like all that mushy stuff!

WE FIRST MET

On the website OKCupid! Rod messaged me first in March of 2016, but I told him that I thought he was too old for me. He ignored this statement and struck up a good conversation with me until I finally agreed to meet him.

Unfortunately, this date was postponed because the night before we were to meet in person, I had a date with someone else and wanted to see where it went (it only lasted about two weeks!)

Five months later, Facebook suggested that I might know Rod and that we could be "friends". After looking at his pictures again I decided to message him and see what he was up to. He immediately responded and asked me out for a drink once more. The rest is history!

16
08.09

OUR FIRST DATE

We met at Mi Cocina, a mexican restaurant, for drinks. Rod was so nervous that he didn't even touch the chips and salsa they brought us or order an iced tea which is his standard order! We talked until the restaurant was about to close and then Rod offered to walk me back to my car and immediately asked for a second date.

16
12.31

HE PROPOSED

On Christmas morning, Rod had surprised me with tickets to see the Broadway musical, The Book of Mormon, at the Winspear Opera House in downtown Dallas. After dinner, we caught the show. He was worried all evening that I'd feel the ring box through his suit coat, but I had no idea he was proposing that night! After the show let out, he guided me out to the reflecting pool in front of the theater and got down on one knee. The answer was YES!
